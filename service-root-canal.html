Why Root Canal?

When the pulp inside a tooth becomes infected, a root canal removes the infection, cleans the canals, and seals them to protect the tooth. This prevents extraction and restores comfort.

  • Painless with local anesthesia
  • Preserves your natural tooth
  • Protects against reinfection
  • Restores chewing function
